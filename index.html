About

Lee En Hao was born in Johor, Malaysia. He earned his B.Eng (Hons) in Telecommunication Engineering from Asia Pacific University of Technology & Innovation, Kuala Lumpur, in 2024. He is currently pursuing a PhD with the Analog, Digital, and RF Research Group at Universiti of Malaya, Kuala Lumpur, focusing on PMICs under the CREST grant.

Previously, he served as a test engineering intern at STMicroelectronics, Muar, Johor and worked as an engineering lab assistant at Asia Pacific University. His research interests are in power management integrated circuit.
