BJT Amplifier

This is an assignment being done for Analogue Electronics module.

Specifications

  • • Total supply voltage: 12 V - 15 V
  • • Input signal voltage: 20 mVpp
  • • Gain: > 42 dB
  • • Lower cut-off frequency: 0 - 100 Hz
  • • Upper cut-off frequency: 500 kHz - 5 MHz
  • • Load resistance: 16 Ω

The amplifier features a three-stage design, with a differential amplifier chosen for its ability to reject common mode noise and produce a smooth sine wave output.

The project repo can be found on analogue circuit.
