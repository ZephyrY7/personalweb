Carbon Sensing Drone

This is a project being done for APICTA 2022.

The Carbon Sensing Drone is a pioneering solution designed to automate the measurement of carbon emission levels across geographical areas. Equipped with advanced sensors and technology, this drone offers efficient data collection for environmental research and management. Its key features include autonomous measurement capabilities, planned route optimization through a mission planner software, and real-time data collection for monitoring environmental changes.

Main Functionalities

  • • Automated carbon measurement
  • • Mission planning and route optimization
  • • Real-time data collection

The drone aims to assess the environmental impact of human activities and aid in developing sustainable solutions. With versatile applications in environmental monitoring, climate change research, or urban planning.
