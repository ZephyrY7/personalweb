Operational Transconductance Amplifier

This is an assignment being done for Analogue Integrated Circuit & Systems module.

Specifications

  • • CMOS Process TSMC 0.18μm
  • • Load capacitance (CL) 10nF
  • • Supply voltage (VDD) ≤ 1.2V
  • • DC power consumption (PDC) ≤ 130μW
  • • Open loop gain (Av, OL) ≥ 80dB
  • • Gain bandwidth product (GBW) ≥ 900kHz
  • • Loop phase margin 60° - 80°
  • • Slew rate ≥ 4mV/μ

Emphasizes current mirror technique for stable biasing across MOS transistors, evaluates Figure-of-Merit (FoM) based on op-amp's gain, GBW, and power consumption. Design constraints include a maximum current of 108μA due to power and voltage limitations.

The design includes a stacking of differential amplifier at the first stage and apply a buffer stage as the output stage.
